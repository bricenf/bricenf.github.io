Synposis:

Market Price = Intrinsic Value + Psychology

And therefore, Psychology = Market Price - Intrinsic Value

The Market Price is what the ticker is traded at and the Intrinsic Value can be found through a DCF or other models



In order to further these ideas to practical application, I needed to develop both hardware and software



Hardware

I needed to build hardware for this project as computation would be nearly 24/7. I needed a device capable of this and needed a device that had the algo trader as its primary application

And I needed something to connect via hardware to an ethernet capable

Therefore, I built upon pre-existing work I did with Raspberry Pi's by purchasing a few more and building a Pi HAT (Hardware Attached on Top) Pi Cluster
