Hi! My name is Bricen, I'm an undergraduate at the City University of New York studying Statistic and Quantitative Modeling at Baruch College and Hunter College. I like learning new things and practically applying them whether it's building out models or creating a website (like this one!). I also love problem solving around any sort of challenge. From simple challenges such as building algorithms to more complex challenge of fixing engines (which you can read about in the Projects page). A lot of my projects revolve around problem solving from how do I better analyze a deck of cards to more practical how do I automate a lawn mower.
